What to Expect

Visiting a new church can be intimidating, but we want you to feel at home. Here's what you can expect when you join us for service.

1

Warm Welcome

Our greeters will welcome you with a smile and help you find your way around.

2

Uplifting Worship

Experience heartfelt praise and worship that will lift your spirit.

3

Powerful Teaching

Hear practical, life-changing messages from God's Word.

4

Genuine Community

Connect with friendly people who will make you feel like family.
