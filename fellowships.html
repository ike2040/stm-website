Building Men of Faith

The Men's Fellowship is a community of men committed to growing in their relationship with God, supporting one another, and becoming godly leaders in their homes, workplaces, and communities.

We meet regularly for Bible study, prayer, mentorship, and fellowship. Our goal is to equip men to live out their faith with integrity, courage, and purpose.

Evangelical Committee

Spreading the Gospel

The Evangelical Committee is a passionate team dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission by sharing the love of Jesus Christ, reaching the lost, and making disciples of all nations.

We organize evangelism campaigns, community outreach programs, and mission trips to bring the gospel to every corner of our society.

Join Evangelical Committee
