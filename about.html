Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Objectives

Mission

To proclaim the true gospel onto salvation to all souls in the church, community, region and the world at large, delivering them from all forms of satanic oppressions and bondages, training and empowering them to become fruitful Disciples of Christ.

Isaiah 61:1-6, Acts 1:8, Jeremiah 1:7-18, Matthew 28:19-20, Mark 16:15-18, John 15:16.

Vision

To become a worldwide recognised ecclesiastical institution with profound knowledge in the pure and true word of God, imbued with the incontestable power of the Holy Ghost and blessed by God with all spiritual blessings in Christ, and endowed with capable workmen and women in the Lord’s vineyard, and a congregation prepared for the second coming of Christ.

Ephesians 1:3-23, Acts 1:8, Isaiah 11:2, I Corinthians 1:5-8, II Corinthians 8:7, I Corinthians 2:12-13, II Corinthians 4:6, II Timothy 2: 20-21, Ephesians 2:10, Colossians 1:9-11, Jeremiah 32:2-4.

"Thy word is truth."

- John 17:17 (KJV)

Our Objectives

1

Gospel & Baptism

To proclaim the true gospel of Jesus Christ to all souls and baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son and of the Holy Ghost.

Matthew 28:18-19, Mark 16:15-16, John 3:5

2

Soul Winning

To win as many souls as possible onto salvation for the kingdom of God.

I Corinthians 9:19-23

3

Liberation

To liberate freely the oppressed from Satanic bondages.

Matthew 10:7-8, Isaiah 42:6-7, Isaiah 61:1-4

4

Empowerment

To empower those who have believed in Jesus Christ to become disciples of the kingdom of God.

Matthew 28:20, John 8:31

5

Restoration

To restore unto all who believe in Jesus Christ and His resurrection power, the joy and peace of God’s salvation with a right and renewed spirit.

Isaiah 61:3-4, Psalm 51:10-12, Isaiah 57:18-19, Isaiah 51:11, Isaiah 49:6

6

Model Church

To be a model church that ministers with the genuine love of God to persons in the community and the world in Jesus’ name.

Acts 10:38, Matthew 5:14-16, Acts 10:38

Total Commitment

To be a church whose purpose is to be Christ-like in our daily living by emphasizing total obedience, commitment of life, personality, and possessions to the Lordship of Christ.

Matthew 11:29, Matthew 5:48, Ephesians 5:1, Joshua 24:24

Our Journey

Our Story

2013

The Beginning

Seekers of the Truth Ministries was founded by Apostle Sammy Nkansah with a small group of believers in Tafo, Kumasi, with a vision to seek and proclaim God's truth.

2015

First Branch

The ministry expanded with the establishment of our first branch in Abetifi, bringing the message of truth to the Eastern Region.

2018

5 Years of Ministry

Celebrated five years of God's faithfulness with growing congregations and expanded ministry impact across Ghana.

2020

Branch Expansion

Seven additional branches were opened in Kasoa, Kwadoagya, Yeji, Nkwanta , Swedru, Atebubu and Diaspora service, extending our reach across Ghana.

Today

Growing Strong

Today, STM continues to grow with over 1,000 members across all branches, remaining committed to seeking and sharing God's truth.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God, the supreme and final authority for all Christian faith and life.

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, co-equal and co-eternal.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ, the Son of God, who was conceived by the Holy Spirit, born of the Virgin Mary, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for our sins, rose from the dead, and will return in glory.

Salvation

We believe that salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, and that all who repent and believe in Him are born again by the Holy Spirit and become children of God.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit, who convicts the world of sin, regenerates believers, indwells and empowers them for godly living and service.

The Church

We believe in the universal Church, the body of Christ, composed of all believers, and in the local church as the expression of this body in a particular community.
