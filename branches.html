Main Campus

Tafo, Kumasi

Our headquarters is located in Tafo, Kumasi, in the Ashanti Region. This is where Apostle Sammy Nkansah ministers, and where our main services are held every Saturday.

The Tafo campus serves as the central hub for all our branches, providing leadership, training, and support to ensure the consistent delivery of God's Word across all our locations.
