About Our Ministry

Welcome to Our Church Family

Seekers of the Truth Ministries is a vibrant Christian community dedicated to seeking, knowing, and sharing the truth of God's Word. Under the leadership of Apostle Sammy Nkansah, we gather every Saturday to worship, learn, and grow together in faith.

Our mission is to proclaim the true gospel onto salvation to all souls in the church, community, region and the world at large, delivering them from all forms of satanic oppressions and bondages, training and empowering them to become fruitful Disciples of Christ.

"And ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free."

- John 8:32 (KJV)
